This is Gamecock Rowing

About Us

The Gamecock Rowing Club is the official club rowing club at the University of South Carolina at Columbia! We practice weekly, training our bodies and our minds for competition with other rowing clubs around the Southeast and beyond. Below is more information related to the club and our activites throughout the year.

Practices

We practice Monday through Saturday. Tuesday and Thursday mornings are in the gym to get us fit and ready to row on the water. Monday, Wednesday, and Fridays are afternoons on the water to refine rowing technique and cohesiveness. Saturday mornings are a long on the water practice to gather and race between the various boats in our club.
